2020: The Most Unbelievable Year

On new years eve 2019, millions of people across the United States were filled with excitement and hope for the new year to come. People celebrated with their eyes on the future, aspiring for 2020 to be better than the 2019 they had just experienced. Unfortunately, it wouldn't be long into the new year before Americans looked back at 2019, longing to return. Within the first three months of 2020 Australian brush fires raged out of control across the continent; legendary NBA player Kobe Bryant died in a tragic helicopter accident; the stock market took two separate 10% dives in on week; and the COVID-19 virus has ravaged the globe. If I could give one piece of advice to the 2019 new years eve party-goer, it would be to turn around and run back to the loving embrace of 2019.
